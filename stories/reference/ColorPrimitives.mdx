## Primitive families in Collection

- `black` (alpha variants)
- `blue`, `cyan`, `neutral`, `orange`, `red`, `green`
- Brand cyan `#0285C8` preserved across rollout

Semantic tokens resolve to primitives. Example: `--button/background/alert` resolves to `#CB2939` (red-alert primitive).
